This app is inspired by the trend "Goodbye to Sora" (a conversation about a video generation tool fading away). Source: https://twitter.com/soraofficialapp/status/2036532795984715896
What is this? A playful replacement for an AI video tool: instead of generating pixels, it generates structure. You get a storyboard, camera plan, and a kinetic preview you can refine and share.
Privacy: everything runs in your browser; exports are local JSON. Share links encode data in the URL hash.
